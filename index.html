Why Home Assistant?

Home Assistant is the ultimate smart home platform for privacy-conscious individuals. With thousands of contributors worldwide, it's open-source and constantly evolving. It offers freedom from being locked into a single vendor, allowing for a vast array of integrations and often enabling you to find the products you need at a much lower price. Whether you're looking to automate your lighting, manage security, or control your media, Home Assistant has you covered. Discover more at Home Assistant.
